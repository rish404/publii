Ladakh & Turtuk, India - A Travel Diary

Turtuk, the last village of India. Situated right on the Pakistan-India border, turtuk is a very less explored destination (and maybe why still so beautiful without a sight of plastic litter). Vehicles are prohibited beyond a point and there's only a bridge connecting people of Turtuk to the other side. These girls wanted some chocolates. We bought them and I got a chance to capture their happiness. And their home!
