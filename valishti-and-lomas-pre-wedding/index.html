Lodhi Garden Humayun Tomb Delhi Pre Wedding Shoot

Doing a pre wedding shoot at Lodhi Gardens & Humayun Tomb is always a great experience. Especially, early morning when the light is soft and great.¬†

This is a great place to capture the beauty of nature.

The Humayun Tomb is a very special place that houses the tomb of Mughal emperor, Shah Jahan. It was built in 1562 by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan after he died. The main attraction of Humayun Tomb is its beautiful architecture and stone carvings. It was built with white marble and rose-coloured bricks, which gives it a very gorgeous look. The construction of Humayun Tomb started in 1560, when Shah Jahan was on his way to Delhi from Agra after the Battle of Panipat where he had defeated Sher Shah Suri who was the ruler of Delhi at that time. He reached Delhi by sea from Cambay and then walked for about 2 months till he reached Humayun Tomb at Faridabad where he stayed for about 4 months after which he started building this magnificent monument to commemorate his visit here on his return journey back to Agra.
