How To Plan A Pre Wedding Shoot At Taj Mahal

If there's one epitome of love in the world, it's Taj Mahal. One of the seven wonders of the world and situated in the Indian city of Agra, there's absolutely no other monument in the world which comes close to the grandness & beauty of Taj Mahal

The mausoleum was commissioned by the Emperor of Mughals Shah Jahan, to house the tomb of his beloved and favourite wife Mumtaz Mahal. And what better location can be there to have a pre wedding shoot with the love of your life other than the Taj itself?

Why Choose Taj Mahal as your pre wedding shoot location?

  • It's a Heritage Monument
  • The surroundings are as beautiful as the monument itself
  • Mehtab Bagh is a stellar vantage point for photos (from where the whole Taj Mahal is visible)
  • You can spend half a day shooting and other half, exploring Taj Mahal

So if you're looking to plan your pre-wedding or engagement shoot at Taj Mahal, Magica By Rish Agarwal is going to be your best option.
